BestPic Dating Profile Feedback Collector

This tool will generate a link so you can receive feedback on your internet dating profile. An example of the end result can be found here. BestPic is entirely open source.

Profile Options

This form will collect all the information needed to create and give feedback on your profile photos and bio.

Album URL

  • All photos must be uploaded directly to an Imgur Album.
  • Do not upload new photos, delete photos, or edit the album once you click submit.
  • Photos must be in an album. You can't use a link directly to a photo on Imgur.
  • Responders will can choose in order up to five photos and share new photo ideas.

Bios

  • Empty inputs will be ignored.
  • Some dating apps limit bios to 500 characters.
  • Responders can only suggest one bio but may edit based on other options.

Instagram

  • Responders can answer yes or no on if you should link your Instagram.
  • Responders can write a short answer with more information.
  • You should consider your privacy before requesting feedback on your Instagram.

Create Link

This will generate a unique link. Make sure to copy it before you leave the page. It is also important to note that you can not edit anything once you click create. You will have to come back, enter each field again, and create a new link.

None of the information you enter into this form can be found online until you share the link.
